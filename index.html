Pastor Chris timeline and impact using God's Word

  • December 7, 1963 - Born Christian Oyakhilome in Edo State, Nigeria
  • Pastor Chris Oyakhilome (D.Sc., D.D.), is the President of LoveWorld Inc. also known as Christ Embassy.
  • He's a healing minister, television host, author and philanthropist
  • He is the author of Rhapsody of Realities - the most translated book in the world.
  • Pastor Chris operates the Inner City Mission Project, which works to assist orphaned and indigent children in inner cities in several countries.
  • His teachings have had a tremendous impact in my life. I started following him since 2003.
  • He has a honorary degree from the Benson Idahosa University, Ambrose Alli University - his alma mata, and Igbinedion University, Okada, all from Nigeria.
  • His message of faith, hope, and love has brought the realities of God's Word to lives of millions around the world.
